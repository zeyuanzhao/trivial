About Us

Trivial is a site for unlimited problem practice and other learning features, based on the vast database of the Art of Problem Solving Wiki. We started it in 2020, after frustration at how inconvenient and expensive preparing for exams like the AMC could be. At its core, Trivial provides tools to efficiently select and work with problems, either individually or in batches. There are various helpful features (loosely inspired by resources like Alcumus and IXL), including answer checking and problem streaks. Filtering and display options are supported for various uses. Attractive article display is also included, including full search and history functionality. All of this can even be printed out, with an optimized layout for offline viewing.
